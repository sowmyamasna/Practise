This city is also known by some as the "City of Pearls," owing to its historical role as a pearl trading center. Nowadays, most of its wealth comes not from jewelry, but information technology. What makes the city special, however, is its ability to meld together its modern vibe with an ancient atmosphere. The Salar Jung Museum and Ramoji Film City, meanwhile, feature diverse art collections and film production facilities.

About this place

At the heart of Agra's allure stands the magnificent Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World. Commissioned in the 17th century by Emperor Shah Jahan in loving memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, the Taj Mahal stands as an epitome of eternal love and architectural brilliance.
